Mac & Cheese

Ingredients
1 stick butter
½ - 1 cup flour
1-2 qaurts milk
1 tsp white pepper
1 tsp salt
1 ½ lb strong cheese, shreaded
2-4 oz brie or other soft cheese
1 large box of pasta


Directions

Melt stick butter in pot. Mix in flour and cook for a couple of minutes until golden/light brown. Add 1-2 qaurts milk, 1 tsp salt and white pepper and cook until thickened a little, about 5 minutes. Add shreaded cheese and brie. Cook, stirring frequently, until about the thickness of chowder, roughly 10 minutes. Mix with cooked pasta.
