Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients
2 pounds beef, no leaner than 90/10
1 ½ teaspoon dill
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon black pepper
4 tablespoons butter
6 green onions, chopped
4 tablespoons flour or cornstarch
2 cups chicken broth
1 ½ teaspoon mustard
16 oz mushrooms, sliced
½ cup sour cream
½ cup white wine

Noodles

2 cups flour
2 teaspoons salt
4 eggs
2 capfuls apple cider vinegar


Directions

Mix all ingredients for the noodles with about a tablespoon of water until dough is together, but not too dry or sticky. Cover and set in refrigerator for at least an hour.

Cut beef into bite-size pieces and season with salt and pepper. In skillet, melt the butter and brown the beef. Push them off to one side. Add onions and mushrooms and cook for 3-5 minutes, then push to the side with beef. Stir in flour into the juices on empty side of pan. Pour in broth and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower the heat and stir in mustard. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes. While meat is simmering, cut noodle dough in half and roll to 1/8 inch thickness on floured surface. Cut with a pizza cutter and boil until al dente, about 10 minutes. Five minutes before serving, stir sour cream, dill and white wine into meat. Heat briefly then serve over noodles.
