Pork Sausage Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

Marinade

Pork Sausage Spaghetti Bolognese
1 zucchini, sliced into half moons
6 oz spaghetti, reserve 1/4 cup pasta water
9 oz pork sausage filling
1 tsp sage, rosemary, & thyme
14oz marinara
Â¼ cup parmesan
1 tbsp butter


Directions

Cook pasta following instructions on box. Cook sausage filling in oiled pan over medium-high heat, about 4-6 minutes. Stir in garlic and spices until fragrant. Add marinara and reduce heat to medium. Simmer until sauce has reduced slightly, about 3-4 minutes. Add spaghetti, pasta water, and butter. Toss to combine. Add zucchini, salt and pepper and cook until zucchini is soft in the center, about 6 minutes. Serve and top with parmesan.
